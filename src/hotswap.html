NDIF Hot-Swapping Pilot Program

NDIF is expanding to host many more models remotely using hot-swapping! See our status page for a preview of some of the models that will be offered through this pilot.

Before publicly launching model hot-swapping, we are seeking innovative minds to help us test our expanded functionalities on their own AI research. This is a unique opportunity for AI researchers to conduct groundbreaking experiments on many different models and their training checkpoints. Participants will help us test our new research infrastructure that provides terabytes of GPU resources and the system support necessary to conduct remote experiments on many different dynamically-loaded models.

What We Offer

  • Be in the first cohort of users to access models beyond our whitelist (including checkpoints)
  • Directly control which models are hosted on the NDIF backend
  • Receive 1:1 research and technical support from NDIF team
  • Training, support, and guidance on the NDIF and NNsight platform
  • Give feedback that will guide future user experience

What We Are Looking For

We are particularly interested in experiments that:

  • Go beyond conventional benchmarking
  • Involve novel interdisciplinary research or model checkpoints
  • Exploit the unique access to model internals that NDIF provides
  • Would otherwise have limited access to the necessary computational resources

We strongly encourage applications from diverse fields that bring unique perspectives to AI research. We especially welcome applications from researchers in U.S. labs aiming to pioneer large-scale AI work at their institutions.
